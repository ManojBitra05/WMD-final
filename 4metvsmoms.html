MET vs MOMA

This document provides an overview of the navigation and filter tools available on the websites of two renowned art institutions, The Metropolitan Museum of Art (The Met) and The Museum of Modern Art (MoMA).
The content highlights the various search filters offered by both institutions, which enable visitors to efficiently locate and explore specific collections, exhibitions, departments, artists, and artworks.
By comparing the user-friendly features of The Met and MoMA's websites, the document aims to help art enthusiasts make the most of their online experience when browsing these prominent museums' digital offerings.
