Bio

I'm a product designer with a passion for user experience and interface design and a full-time student currently enrolled in Master's in User Experience design and development at NYIT for Fall'22.

I'm currently living in Manhattan, Newyork where I work at NYIT as a VR and Game design Graduate assistant under Prof. Kevin Park. My hobbies include playing piano , cooking , swimming , and obsessing over anything design .

I value storytelling while considering human-centered design, so I’m able to explore new ways products can connect with people. I'm constantly gathering inspiration and searching for innovative ideas.
