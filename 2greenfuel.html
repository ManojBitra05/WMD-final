Green fuel

This design guideline document provides a systematic and detailed roadmap for employing cutting-edge valorization technologies that transform food waste into a diverse range of biofuels, including biodiesel, bioethanol, biohydrogen, bio-oil, biochar, and biomethane.
It emphasizes the significance of these approaches in addressing the global energy crisis and promoting a sustainable bioeconomy.

Vision

The goal of our project is to convert the food waste to non-renewable energy biofuel.

Our vision is to gather the food waste from houses and farms, and to reproduce them into a new energy resource called Biofuel.
Users will gain several benefits using our app, along with landfilling the waste.

Design Tone

These guidelines are underpinned by research and an established visual foundation that informs every element.
